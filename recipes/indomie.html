Noodles

Description

Noodles have been able to capture the hearts of most Nigerians as it serves as a simple breakfast and it goes through an easy-to-make process.

Ingredients for making noodles:

  • 2 Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh pepper
  • Eggs
  • 50ml vegetable oil

Steps in making noodles:

  1. Put the indomie in a bowl and pour boiled water into it, allowing for about five minutes for the noodles to get soft.
  2. Set your frying pan on the heat, add sliced tomatoes, onions, fresh pepper, and fry for about five minutes as well.
  3. Drain water from the noodles after 4 minutes, and add the follow-come spices while still in the bowl, the noodles will be both soft and hot.
  4. Break the two eggs into the same bowl containing the noodles and stir together, then transfer to the frying pan.
  5. Cook and stir for about five minutes
